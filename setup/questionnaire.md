# Workspace Onboarding Questionnaire

Answer these questions at the start of a new run or when onboarding a new contributor.

## Project basics

- What is the project?
- What is the immediate goal of this run?
- Who are the users or stakeholders?
- What outputs are expected?

## Tools and environment

- What tools are used? (Android Studio, Node.js, PostgreSQL, etc.)
- What models or agents may be used?
- What files already exist that are relevant?
- What must not be changed?

## Conventions

- What are the naming conventions? (see `_config/naming-conventions.md`)
- What knowledge should persist beyond this run?
- What is sensitive or secret?

## Success and next steps

- What does success look like?
- What should the next agent do first?
- What open decisions need a human answer?
