# Passport Sections

The district core passport has four sections:

| Code | Title | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| `ENTERED_APPRENTICE` | Entered Apprentice | Active after initiation date is recorded. |
| `FELLOW_CRAFT` | Fellow Craft | Unlocks after passing date / approved transition. |
| `MASTER_MASON` | Master Mason and Beyond | Unlocks after raising date / approved transition. |
| `PREPARING_FOR_OFFICE` | Preparing for Office | Unlocks after Master Mason section completion or Lodge configuration. |

## Section contents

Each section contains:

- learning outcomes
- additional items
- mentor session targets
- visitation targets
- rituals performed targets
- dates (initiated, passed, raised, completed)
- mentor sign-off

## Rules

- Future sections may be visible as a locked roadmap but must not be editable until unlocked.
- Lodge supplements may exist but must not break district comparability.
- Section completion requires verified milestones, required counts, and recorded sign-off.
